How to Play
Congratulations you're rich enough to be able to afford at least seven of a celebrities' dirty and used items!
The object of the game is to auction any of the cards in your hand you don't want, decieve your buyer, and blind bid on items based on their value. The player with the most points in their hand at the end wins. In this game it's okay to lie.
